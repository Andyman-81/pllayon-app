Title: "0.1 VISION"
Intro text (small, grey, italic):
  "This is not about rankings or trophies. It is about the kind of athlete
   and person you want to become. Answer honestly — these answers drive
   everything that follows."

Fields:

1. label: "What does success look like for me in 12 months?"
   hint: "Think beyond results. What kind of athlete do you want to be?"
   placeholder: "e.g. I want to compete at state level and understand why I win or lose — not just whether I do."
   db_field: vision_12_months

2. label: "What does success look like in 24 months?"
   hint: "Where is the journey heading?"
   placeholder: "e.g. I want my first national ranking event and to feel physically ready to compete at that level."
   db_field: vision_24_months

3. label: "Why does this matter to me — not to my coach or my parents, but to me?"
   hint: "This is the most important question in this module. Take your time."
   placeholder: "e.g. I want to prove to myself that I can develop into the athlete I know I can be. Not for anyone else — for me."
   db_field: vision_why

4. label: "What kind of athlete do I want to be known as?"
   hint: "Not what you want to achieve — who you want to be."
   placeholder: "e.g. Someone who competes hard, learns fast and handles pressure well."
   db_field: vision_identity

MODULE 0.2 — STANDARDS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Title: "0.2 STANDARDS"
Intro text:
  "Your standards are the non-negotiables. They are what you do every day —
   not what you do when you feel like it."

Fields:

1. label: "What are my three non-negotiable training standards?"
   hint: "These are things you will always do, regardless of how you feel."
   placeholder: "e.g. 1. I arrive on time and ready to train. 2. I complete every drill at full effort. 3. I never leave without reviewing one thing I learned."
   db_field: standards_training

2. label: "What standard do I hold myself to in competition?"
   hint: "Not about winning — about how you compete."
   placeholder: "e.g. I compete hard on every point regardless of the score. I control what I can control and reset after mistakes."
   db_field: standards_competition

3. label: "What standard do I hold myself to in recovery?"
   hint: "Sleep, nutrition, how you treat your body between sessions."
   placeholder: "e.g. I hit my sleep target at least 5 nights a week. I eat before training. I don't skip cooldowns."
   db_field: standards_recovery

4. label: "What is the one standard I most need to improve right now?"
   hint: "Be honest. This is private."
   placeholder: "e.g. Showing up mentally when I'm tired. I switch off in the last 20 minutes of long sessions."
   db_field: standards_improve

Title: "0.3 ENVIRONMENT"
Intro text:
  "Your development is shaped by the environment around you — not just
   your talent. Map it honestly."

Fields:

1. label: "What does my training environment currently do well?"
   hint: "Think about your coach, facility, training partners and schedule."
   placeholder: "e.g. My coach gives good technical feedback and we have court time 4 days a week. My training partner pushes me."
   db_field: env_strengths

2. label: "What is missing or holding me back in my current environment?"
   hint: "Be specific. Vague answers produce vague plans."
   placeholder: "e.g. I don't have enough competitive match play. I train well but don't get enough pressure situations."
   db_field: env_gaps

3. label: "Who in my environment helps my development the most — and how?"
   hint: "Could be a coach, parent, teammate, mentor."
   placeholder: "e.g. My S&C coach — she tracks my physical progress properly and adjusts based on data, not guesswork."
   db_field: env_support

4. label: "What one change to my environment would make the biggest difference?"
   hint: "Something realistic that could actually happen."
   placeholder: "e.g. More competitive matches against players slightly better than me. I learn more when I'm being pushed."
   db_field: env_change

MODULE 0.4 — ROUTINES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Title: "0.4 ROUTINES"
Intro text:
  "Routines remove the need for willpower. The best athletes do not rely
   on motivation — they rely on habits that run automatically."

Fields:

1. label: "What is my current morning routine on training days?"
   hint: "Walk through what you actually do — not what you wish you did."
   placeholder: "e.g. Wake at 7am, breakfast within 30 min, leave by 8:15. Usually check phone too long before getting up — that needs to change."
   db_field: routine_morning

2. label: "What is my pre-training routine?"
   hint: "From when you arrive to when training starts."
   placeholder: "e.g. Arrive 15 min early, dynamic warm-up, 5 min hitting to settle in. I want to add a mental prep habit — not sure what yet."
   db_field: routine_pretraining

3. label: "What is my post-training routine?"
   hint: "Recovery, nutrition, reviewing the session."
   placeholder: "e.g. Cooldown stretch (10 min), protein shake, write one thing I worked on in my notes. Then off court within 30 min."
   db_field: routine_posttraining

4. label: "What is my pre-competition routine?"
   hint: "The night before and the morning of a match."
   placeholder: "e.g. Still figuring this out. I know I need to sleep earlier the night before but I don't always do it."
   db_field: routine_precomp

5. label: "Which routine needs the most work right now?"
   hint: "One honest answer."
   placeholder: "e.g. Post-training. I rush off court without recovering properly and it affects the next day."
   db_field: routine_weakest

Title: "0.5 OBSTACLES"
Intro text:
  "Every athlete faces obstacles. The ones who develop fastest are the ones
   who can name their obstacles accurately and plan for them in advance."

Fields:

1. label: "What is the biggest obstacle to my development right now?"
   hint: "Could be physical, mental, circumstantial or environmental."
   placeholder: "e.g. School workload. Term 3 is always brutal and my training drops off. I haven't found a way to manage it yet."
   db_field: obstacle_primary

2. label: "What obstacles come up repeatedly that slow me down?"
   hint: "Patterns — not one-off events."
   placeholder: "e.g. I lose focus in long sessions. After about 75 minutes my decision-making drops and I stop competing hard."
   db_field: obstacle_recurring

3. label: "What do I do when things get hard — honestly?"
   hint: "Your actual response — not the response you wish you had."
   placeholder: "e.g. I get quiet and withdraw. I stop trying new things and just go through the motions until the session ends."
   db_field: obstacle_response

4. label: "What is my plan for when my most common obstacle shows up this program?"
   hint: "Specific — not 'try harder'. WHEN it happens, I will..."
   placeholder: "e.g. When I feel myself switching off mid-session, I will stop, take a breath, and set one specific micro-goal for the next 10 minutes."
   db_field: obstacle_plan
