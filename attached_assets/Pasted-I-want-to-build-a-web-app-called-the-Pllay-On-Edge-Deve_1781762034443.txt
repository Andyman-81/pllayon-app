I want to build a web app called the Pllay On Edge Development Program — Athlete Edition.

This is a 12-week athlete development tracking app for young athletes aged 10–18.

Use the following as your complete design and content specification. Do not copy the HTML directly — rebuild it as a proper web app with persistent data storage, user accounts and a clean mobile-first UI.

---

TECH STACK

- Frontend: React + Tailwind CSS
- Backend: Supabase (auth + database)
- Hosting: Vercel or Replit deployment
- No external UI libraries — build clean custom components

USER FLOW

1. Athlete creates account (name, DOB, sport, club, coach name, parent name)
2. They are placed into Phase 0 — Establishment
3. They complete Phase 0 modules before Week 1 unlocks
4. Each Sunday, Week N unlocks and the weekly reflection is available
5. After Week 4, Month 1 Check-In unlocks
6. After Week 8, Month 2 Check-In unlocks
7. After Week 12, Capstone unlocks
8. All data is saved to Supabase and persists across sessions

APP STRUCTURE — SCREENS

1. ONBOARDING / REGISTRATION
   - Full-screen dark cover (black #111111)
   - Green accent strip at top
   - "PLLAY ON / EDGE PROGRAM" in Barlow Condensed
   - "Athlete Edition" label in green
   - Form fields: name, DOB, sport, club, coach, parent
   - "Begin Program" button in green
   - Saves to Supabase athletes table

2. DASHBOARD (home screen after login)
   - Top: athlete name + current phase badge (coloured by phase)
   - Phase progress bar showing which phase they are in
   - Current week card (large, prominent)
   - Quick stats: weeks completed, reflections done, completion %
   - Phase timeline strip at bottom (5 phases, colour coded)
   - Locked weeks shown as greyed out

3. PHASE 0 — ESTABLISHMENT (must complete before Week 1)
   - Module 0.1: Assessment Review
     - Maturity stage selector (Pre-PHV / Circa-PHV / Post-PHV)
     - Domain scorecard: Physical, Technical, Tactical, Psychological, Lifestyle — each scored /10 with target and gap
     - Text fields: strengths, development areas, #1 priority
   - Module 0.2: Competitive Advantage Map
     - Same 5-domain table with priority action field
   - Module 0.3: Profile + Values + Support Team
     - Profile fields (auto-filled from registration where possible)
     - Values: 3 value fields each with training/competition description
     - Support team table: role / name / what I need from them
   - Module 0.4: My Goals
     - 3 outcome goal fields
     - 2 behaviour goals each with WHEN / WHERE / HOW fields
   - Module 0.5: Tennis Roadmap — Pathway Milestones
     - Checklist of 11 milestones, each tappable with date field
     - Trajectory fields: 12 months, 24 months
   - Module 0.6: Commitment
     - Digital signature (typed name as confirmation)
     - Date
     - "Start Week 1" button — only appears when all modules complete

4. WEEKLY REFLECTION (one per week, unlocks each Sunday)
   - Header: "WEEK N" large in phase colour
   - Phase label and date range
   - Mission box (coloured by phase, pre-filled with that week's mission text)
   - Scorecard: 5 dimensions, 1–5 tap-to-select
   - 3 text fields: best moment, biggest challenge, key learning
   - Focus question (pre-filled, different each week)
   - Answer field
   - Implementation intention: WHEN / WHERE / HOW
   - Save button → marks week complete

   Week missions and focus questions (pre-fill these in the app):
   Week 1 mission: "Complete every planned session. Do not miss one."
   Week 1 question: "What is one habit I performed consistently this week without being reminded?"
   Week 2 mission: "Track your recovery. Log your sleep every morning."
   Week 2 question: "What did I do in training this week that I would not have done six months ago?"
   Week 3 mission: "Show up to every session with a written training intention."
   Week 3 question: "Where did I take ownership of my development without being told?"
   Week 4 mission: "Complete your Month 1 Check-In at the end of this week."
   Week 4 question: "What small change to my daily routine would make next week better?"
   Week 5 mission: "Seek one difficult training moment this week — and study it."
   Week 5 question: "What was the hardest moment this week and what did it reveal?"
   Week 6 mission: "After every session, write one challenge and what you tried."
   Week 6 question: "Describe a mistake you made. What did you try? What did you learn?"
   Week 7 mission: "Identify your most common match mistake. Create a practice scenario to face it."
   Week 7 question: "If you could change one competitive behaviour right now, what would it be?"
   Week 8 mission: "Complete a full competition review. Do not skip the decision section."
   Week 8 question: "Looking at the last four weeks: what pattern do you notice?"
   Week 9 mission: "Write your performance plan before your most important session or match."
   Week 9 question: "What does competing at your best actually look like — specifically?"
   Week 10 mission: "Identify one competitive behaviour to express in your next match."
   Week 10 question: "Describe a decision you made under pressure. Was it right?"
   Week 11 mission: "Design and test your pre-competition routine this week."
   Week 11 question: "What separates the athletes you want to beat from those you struggle against?"
   Week 12 mission: "Complete the Capstone preparation. Review Months 1 and 2."
   Week 12 question: "In 12 weeks, what has changed about how you approach your development?"

5. COMPETITION REVIEW (available after any match, minimum 1 per phase)
   - Match details: competition name, date, opponent, result
   - 3 rating rows (1–5): overall performance, decision quality, emotional control
   - Text fields: best decision, decision to change, key learning
   - Implementation intention: WHEN / WHERE / HOW

6. MONTH 1 CHECK-IN (unlocks after Week 4 completion)
   - Scorecard averages for 4 dimensions (auto-calculated from Weeks 1–4 if data exists, otherwise manual)
   - Goal progress table: goal / progress / status
   - Text fields: key habit built, key learning, one change for Phase 2
   - Coach notes field (read-only prompt: "your coach will complete this separately")

7. MONTH 2 CHECK-IN (unlocks after Week 8 completion)
   - Scorecard for: resilience, adaptability, confidence, ownership of mistakes
   - Goal progress update
   - Text fields: most valuable learning, how I've changed, main challenge for Phase 3
   - Setback log: what happened / what I tried / what I learned (optional)

8. PRE-COMPETITION PLAN (available in Phase 3)
   - Match details: competition, date, opponent
   - Text fields: primary tactic, emotional target, trigger points, plan for those moments
   - Routine table: evening before / morning of / court warm-up physical / court warm-up technical / mental prep
   - Pressure plan field

9. CAPSTONE REVIEW (unlocks after Week 12 completion)
   - Domain comparison: 5 domains, start vs now vs change
   - Goal achievement: 3 goals with achieved/partially/not yet + evidence
   - Evidence fields: competition result, technical improvement, behaviour change
   - Field tests: 2 yes/no questions + notes field
   - 4-dimension self-rating scorecard
   - Reflection fields: proud moment, fell short, key learning, do differently
   - Digital signature + date

10. APPENDICES (always accessible from dashboard)
    - Appendix A: Dynamic Warm-Up Protocol (static content, 3-step: mobility / activation / CNS)
    - Appendix B: Gym S&C Session (static content, 4 blocks: power / lower / upper / injury prevention)
    - Appendix C: Body Management (sleep log interactive — 7 days, rate each night; nutrition protocol static)
    - Appendix D: Cooldown Protocol (static content, 3-step: breathing / stretching / session log)
    - Sleep log in Appendix C should save to Supabase weekly

11. PROGRESS SCREEN
    - Overall completion % 
    - Weeks completed vs total
    - Reflections streak (consecutive Sundays)
    - Domain scores chart (start vs current — bar chart)
    - Competition reviews count
    - Milestones achieved checklist

UX RULES

- Mobile-first. Designed for a phone screen. All inputs large enough to tap comfortably.
- Phase colour system is the primary navigation signal. Every screen shows the current phase colour.
- Locked content is visible but greyed out with a lock icon — not hidden entirely.
- Every form auto-saves as the user types (debounced 2 seconds).
- Completed items show a green tick. Incomplete show an empty circle.
- The weekly reflection must show the current week's mission prominently — it is the most important UI element on that screen.
- Implementation intention fields (WHEN / WHERE / HOW) always appear as a grouped trio with grey background.
- Never use motivational language. Direct and evidence-based only.
- No emojis in content. Clean, modern, premium.

---

CONTENT RULES

- All mission text and focus questions are pre-loaded in the app (listed above) — the athlete cannot edit them
- The athlete's written responses are the only user-generated content
- Coach name is displayed as a reference but coaches do not have app access in this version (V1)
- Parent name is stored but parents do not have app access in V1
- All data belongs to the athlete account
